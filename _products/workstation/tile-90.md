**TILE 90 product infomation**

- 90mm THICKNESS PANEL provides the highest panel capacity, allowing easy access, design flexibilty as well as generous space for users with extra
  cabling needs.
- TILE system allows easy access for maintenance and repair work at specific problem areas without dismantling the whole configuration.
- Integrated cable management helps keep the worksurface clear and clutter-free.
- Cables can be easily accessed from both front and back, the extra wide raceway provides generous space for users with extra cabling needs.
- A full of range of accessories are available and can be added on anytime.
