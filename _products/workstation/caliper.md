**CALIPER product infomation**

- Our simplest desking system for the user that requires a no-frills solution.
- Our systems can be dismantled, repositioned and rebuilt within minutes, enabling easy reconfiguration or relocation of project teams or departments.
- Modular construction enables quick and easy installation, supports quick routing of data and power cabling.
- Modular elements allows creative and diverse configurations such as long workbenches, face-to-face workstations and individual.
- Integrated cable management helps keep the worksurface clear and clutter-free.
- The thin 16mm fabric panel profile and simple style optimizes use of space.
- A full of range of accessories are available and can be added on anytime.
