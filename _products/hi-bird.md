**Hi-BRID product infomation**

- The combination of TILE 90 & FIN 30 to provide a solution that allows for robust cabling needs as well as small lightweight footprint.
- 90mm TILE 90 acts as a spine, allowing easy access, design flexibilty as well as generous space for users with extra cabling needs.
- 30mm FIN 30 provides a lightweight aesthetic and space-saving benefits.
- Integrated cable management helps keep the worksurface clear and clutter-free.
- Cables can be easily accessed from both front and back, the extra wide raceway provides generous space for users with extra cabling needs.
- A full of range of accessories are available and can be added on anytime.
